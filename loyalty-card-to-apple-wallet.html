Frequently Asked Questions

Can I add loyalty cards to Apple Wallet?

Yes. PassX can create Wallet-compatible passes from loyalty card barcodes or QR codes on iPhone.

Can I create store cards for Apple Wallet?

Yes. Store cards and rewards cards are common PassX use cases when they include a barcode or QR code.

Will the card work at checkout?

Often it can, but it depends on the scanner, store policy, barcode type, screen scanning support, and the merchant system.

Is this the same as an official store app pass?

No. A PassX-created pass is not an official store app pass. It is a Wallet-compatible pass you create from your own card code.
