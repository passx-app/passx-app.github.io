Frequently Asked Questions

Can I add a barcode to Apple Wallet?

Yes. PassX can create Wallet-compatible passes from barcodes so you can store them in Apple Wallet on iPhone.

What types of barcodes can I use?

PassX is intended for common scannable card, coupon, and ticket barcodes. Compatibility can depend on the barcode type and the merchant scanner.

Can I add loyalty card barcodes?

Yes. Loyalty cards and store rewards cards are common use cases for PassX.

Will every barcode work with every store?

No guarantee. PassX can create a Wallet-compatible pass, but final usability depends on the scanner, store policy, barcode type, and merchant system.
